## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you meet these basic requirements to get started smoothly.

<Callout kind="info">

- A valid email address for account creation and verification
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- Basic personal information ready for profile setup (age, gender, health goals)

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up using your preferred method. Star Medical Wellness supports both email registration and social sign-ins for quick access.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Signup" icon="mail">
    Visit `https://app.starmedicalwellness.com/signup` and follow these steps:

    1. Enter your email address.
    2. Create a strong password (at least 12 characters with symbols).
    3. Agree to the terms and click **Create Account**.

    You'll receive a confirmation email immediately.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google Sign-in" icon="google">
    Click the **Sign in with Google** button on the signup page.

    - Select your Google account.
    - Grant permissions for email and profile access.
    - Your account auto-creates with Google details pre-filled.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Verify Email and Complete Profile

After signup, check your inbox for the verification email from Star Medical Wellness.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="check-circle">
    Click the verification link in the email. This activates your account and unlocks full features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="user">
    Log in at `https://app.starmedicalwellness.com/login`.

    Fill out your profile:

    | Field          | Details                          |
    |----------------|----------------------------------|
    | Full Name      | Your legal name                  |
    | Date of Birth  | For personalized recommendations |
    | Wellness Goals | Select from weight loss, hormones, labs, aesthetics |
    | Contact Info   | Phone for appointment reminders  |

    Upload a profile photo if desired, then save changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Accurate profile data enables tailored wellness plans based on integrative root-cause medicine.
</Callout>
