## Getting Started

1. Visit `https://starmedicalwellness.com`
2. Click "Sign Up" at the top right
3. Fill in your personal and contact information
4. Choose a program: weight loss, lab panel, hormones, or aesthetics
5. Schedule your initial consultation in the portal
6. Complete your medical history forms

## Need help?
Email us at `support@starmedicalwellness.com`. We’ll guide you through your first steps.
