## Online Booking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Go to `https://starmedicalwellness.com/portal` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Appointments">
    Click the "Appointments" tab in the portal menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your provider and service">
    Choose your desired practitioner and service (e.g., weight loss, labs).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a date and time">
    Browse available slots and select a time that works for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click "Book Appointment". You’ll receive a confirmation email shortly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
