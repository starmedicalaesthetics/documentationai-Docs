---
title: Cannot Access the Patient Portal
description: Quick steps if you can't log in to the Star Medical Wellness portal.
---

## Symptoms

- Homepage loads, but login fails
- "Invalid credentials" error

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click "Forgot Password?" and check your email for instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear cache, then try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Make sure your registered email is correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If the issue persists, email us at `support@starmedicalwellness.com`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
